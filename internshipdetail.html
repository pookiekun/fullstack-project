Software Engineering Intern

Stripe • San Francisco, CA • $50/hr - $65/hr

Internship Description

Join Stripe's core infrastructure team to design, build, and maintain the next generation of global payment systems. This is a unique opportunity to work on highly scalable, distributed systems that power millions of businesses worldwide. You will collaborate with experienced engineers to tackle complex challenges in reliability, scalability, and performance, making a direct impact on our product and users.

Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and deploy backend services with a focus on high availability, low latency, and scalability.
  • Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code in languages such as Go, Ruby, or Java.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including product managers, designers, and other engineers to deliver new features.
  • Participate in code reviews to maintain a high-quality code culture.
  • Troubleshoot and debug production issues across services and multiple levels of the stack.

Qualifications

  • Currently pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
  • Strong understanding of computer science fundamentals, including data structures, algorithms, and software design.
  • Experience with one or more general-purpose programming languages (e.g., Python, Java, Go, C++).
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work independently or as part of a team.
  • Familiarity with distributed systems and cloud computing (AWS, GCP, Azure) is a plus.

About the Company

Stripe is a technology company that builds economic infrastructure for the internet. Businesses of every size—from new startups to public companies—use our software to accept payments and manage their businesses online. We're on a mission to increase the GDP of the internet, and we believe that by making it easier for entrepreneurs to start, run, and scale their businesses, we can make a meaningful impact on the world.
